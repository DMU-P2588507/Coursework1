Bill Skarsgård

Bill Skarsgård is a Swedish actor born on 9th August,1990. He is the son of actor Stellan Starsgård. Skarsgård plays Pennywise in both IT and IT chapter 2. His first major American film was Divergent in 2016, with IT coming out in 2017. Critics and fans praised him for his role as the dancing clown.
