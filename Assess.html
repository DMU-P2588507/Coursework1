Synopsis:

Set in Derry 1988, It tells the story of seven young outcasts, facing their own fears and nightmares against the supernatural clown - Pennywise. The clown is known to appear and cause terror to people of Derry every 27 years by preying on the fears of each victim individually. Once one of the children, Bill Denberough, loses his brother to the sewer dwelling clown, the film follows him and the rest of 'The Losers Club', as they try to stop him for good.
